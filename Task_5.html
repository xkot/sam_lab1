Have you seen this dangerous monster?

A racoon woke up thousands of Berliners and prompted hundreds of anxious calls to police after it triggered a thunderous explosion at a power station.

The animal broke into the facility and caused a short circuit in a 110,000-volt transformer at 2.10am yesterday. The ensuing blast could be heard up to four miles from the plant in the western neighbourhood of Siemensstadt.
